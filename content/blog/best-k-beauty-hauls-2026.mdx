A great K-Beauty haul balances tried-and-true staples with one or two playful impulse buys. This post is for anyone who wants the dopamine of a YesStyle unboxing without the regret.

## The haul formula

1. One gentle cleanser
2. One hydrating toner or essence
3. One serum for your main concern
4. One lip tint or cushion compact
5. One cute accessory or tool

This gives you enough to build a routine while leaving room for something fun.
