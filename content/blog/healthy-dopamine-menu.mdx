A healthy dopamine menu is a list of small activities that reliably boost your mood without the crash. Instead of relying on [oddly satisfying websites](/sites/oddly-satisfying) or impulse shopping every time you feel flat, you choose from a menu of proven options.

## What goes on the menu?

Good menu items are fast, low-effort, and genuinely rewarding. Examples include:

- A five-minute walk outside
- Making a cup of tea
- Stretching or dancing to one song
- Texting a friend
- Looking at photos from a happy memory

## Why it beats doom-scrolling

Social media gives you unpredictable dopamine hits. Sometimes you get a great post, sometimes nothing. A dopamine menu gives you reliable, controllable rewards that do not leave you feeling drained.
