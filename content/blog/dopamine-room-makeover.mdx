A dopamine room makeover does not require a big budget. It requires intention. The goal is to fill your space with colors, textures, and objects that make you feel good every time you see them.

## Before: the blank canvas

Most rooms start neutral — white walls, beige furniture, minimal personality. There is nothing wrong with neutral, but it rarely sparks joy. The first step is choosing one or two colors that make you smile.

## After: color + texture + memory

Add a colorful throw pillow, a patterned rug, and one framed photo or souvenir from a happy trip. Layer in plants or flowers for living energy. Finish with warm lighting.
