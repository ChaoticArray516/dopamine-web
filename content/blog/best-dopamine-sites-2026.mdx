We have rounded up the best dopamine sites to visit when bored — from fake shopping to interactive art to oddly satisfying toys.

## The Useless Web

[The Useless Web](/sites/the-useless-web) is the original random-website generator. One button press sends you somewhere pointless, surprising, and usually delightful. It never gets old because you never know what is coming.

## DopamineShopping.com

If you love the feeling of adding things to a cart but hate spending money, [DopamineShopping.com](/sites/dopamineshopping) is your fix. It looks like a real store, but nothing ever ships.

## Pixel Thoughts

For a calmer kind of dopamine, [Pixel Thoughts](/sites/pixel-thoughts) turns your current worry into a shrinking star. Sixty seconds later, your stress feels a little lighter.

## Pointer Pointer

[Pointer Pointer](/sites/pointer-pointer) is interactive art at its silliest. Move your cursor and a photo of someone pointing at it appears. It is weirdly satisfying.

## Window Swap

Need a change of scenery? [Window Swap](/sites/window-swap) lets you look out of a stranger's window somewhere in the world. It is cozy, quiet, and perfect for a mental reset.
